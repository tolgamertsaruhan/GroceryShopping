# GroceryShopping

It is a Java-based shopping simulator application that provides a user-friendly interface for buying various products such as food and beverages. The platform aims to meet the needs of both customers and administrators by offering a seamless shopping experience and efficient product catalog management.

## Features

### Customer-Facing Features
- View product details, including features and prices.
- Benefit from special discounts for bulk purchases or exceeding a certain price limit.

### Admin-Facing Features
- Manage the product catalog.
- Add new products.
- Remove old products.

## Usage

### Customer Mode
1. Browse through the available products.
2. Add desired items to the shopping cart.
3. Register your credit card in the system and make payment.
4. Receive your invoice at the end of your shopping.

### Admin Mode
1. Log in as an authorized administrator.
2. Manage the product catalog by adding or removing products.
3. Monitor and update inventory levels.
